## Text Overlay Patterns

When writing [[text-overlays]] for Sophie:

**POV openers** (on the hand-over-mouth clip):
- Lowercase, dry, slightly deadpan
- Pattern: "pov: [mundane setup] + [surprisingly real outcome]"
- Examples:
  - "pov: you replaced 3 minutes of scrolling with manifesting and now you have a 21-day streak"
  - "pov: an app locked your phone until you read your affirmations out loud"
  - "my screen time after 30 days of manifesting every morning:"

**Reaction text** (on the smiling/reacting clip):
- Short, understated, the humor is in the restraint
- Pattern: "[matter-of-fact observation]" or "[reluctant admission]"
- Examples: "the app ate", "okay fine it works", "i'm not even mad", "well that happened", "noted."
